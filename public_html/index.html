About Me

I am a Front-End Developer specializing in React and developed a project using MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js) with a passion for building scalable and user-friendly web applications.

I have successfully completed a Postgraduate Certificate in Computer Application Development at Conestoga College, Canada, and I am currently pursuing a Postgraduate Certificate in Reporting Systems and Database Development.

With over 8 years of professional experience, including 2+ years in technical roles, I have developed applications that address real-world challenges, honing my expertise in technical support, web development, requirement gathering, MERN stack development, testing, and deployment. My capstone project, Indie Fusion, is a web platform designed to empower indie artists by enabling collaboration, event creation, and talent showcasing. I have a strong track record of delivering end-to-end solutions with a focus on efficiency, scalability, and user experience.

Beyond technical expertise, I have 6+ years experience in non-technical roles, I have transitioned from a Process Associate to a Management Trainee, gaining strong leadership and management skills, including team management, training and mentoring team members, project coordination, stakeholder communication, and process optimization. I have successfully led teams, streamlined workflows, and contributed to process improvements that enhanced efficiency and team productivity.
